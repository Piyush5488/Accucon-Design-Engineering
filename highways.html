A Highways and Bridges
(Including drainage design)

Highways and Bridges are some of the most important infrastructure when it comes to connecting everyone to the world around us. But across the world today, road users, operators and decision makers face many evolving challenges related to road design & performance owing to increasing driver numbers, constrained budgets, asset resilience, road user safety and customer's service expectations.

At ADE we provides innovative solutions in the field of Highways using Modern Technology, and extensive Computer Applications for Survey, Planning, Design, Construction Supervision of 2/4/6/8 lane Highways, Expressways, Urban Roads, Hill Roads and other Road Networks. ADE also offers services related to Structures, Bridges, and Flyovers. We work for Government as well as private clients and offer services ranging from Pre bid, Feasibility stage report, detail project report and detail engineering for execution of work.

ADE has also endeavored to bring in innovation to its projects. To empower these work standards, we have setup state-of-the-art infrastructure. Internationally acclaimed and accepted software like MX Roads, Open Roads, Civil 3D, IIT Pave, Staad Pro and AutoCAD form the core of our design and drafting departments.
